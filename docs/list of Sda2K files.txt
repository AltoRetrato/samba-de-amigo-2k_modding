By Ricardo Mendonça Ferreira - ric@mpcnet.com.br

2018.10.19 - First (almost) complete release.
2018.10.06 - First draft.

Obs.:
 - My knowledge of the Japanese language is limited!
 - So is my experience with Dreamcast development!
 - What I'm unsure of is marked with a question mark.
 - Song titles without their respective audio and beatmap files are marked with an asterisk.
 - Most translations were brought directly from the Wii version.
 - Take everything with a grain of salt (and watch your blood pressure)!
 - Corrections and suggestions are welcome!
